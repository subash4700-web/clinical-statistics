🧪 Dance of the p-values

Repeat experiments under fixed true parameters — watch how CIs and p-values vary, and how p < α fraction estimates power.

Sample size & power

Exercise 4
False positives (H₀ is true)
When there is no true effect, roughly 5% of experiments will still be significant — that is α in action.
μc = 50 μe = 50 σ = 20 n = 50 δ = 0.00
Exercise 5
Statistical vs clinical significance
A trivially small difference becomes highly significant with a large enough sample — but is it meaningful?
