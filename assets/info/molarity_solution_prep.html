Molarity & Solution Preparation

This tool supports two common workflows:

  • Make from solid: calculate how many grams to weigh to prepare a solution at a target molarity.

Make from Solid (Grams)

Core relationship:

moles = M × V
grams = moles × MW

  • M in mol/L (M)
  • V in liters (L)
  • MW in g/mol

Dilution from Stock

Use when you have a concentrated stock and need a weaker working solution:

C₁ × V₁ = C₂ × V₂

  • C₁: stock concentration
  • V₁: volume of stock to add
  • C₂: target concentration
  • V₂: final total volume

Lab Notes

  • Double-check units (M vs mM vs µM; L vs mL vs µL).
  • Very small pipetted volumes can be error-prone; consider preparing an intermediate dilution.
  • For clinical reagents, follow SOPs for labeling, stability, and storage conditions.

Educational use only; always verify with SOPs and validated methods.
