Color intensity represents cell counts. Discordant cells (b and c, highlighted) determine the test outcome.

Accuracy & limitations: McNemar's test is based only on the discordant pairs (cells b and c) — concordant pairs (a and d) do not affect the p-value. The chi-square approximation is reliable when b + c ≥ 25. With fewer discordant pairs, use the exact binomial version of McNemar's test for a more accurate p-value.
