Observed vs Expected Counts

Comparison of observed counts (blue) and expected counts (orange) for each cell in the 2×2 table.

Accuracy & limitations: The chi-square approximation is valid when all expected cell counts are ≥ 5. Expected counts are shown in the table above; any cell below 5 is flagged. For 2×2 tables with low expected counts, use Fisher's Exact Test instead — it gives an exact p-value regardless of sample size.
