Colony Count — CFU Calculator BETA

Calculate colony forming units (CFU) per mL or g from dilution series plate counts

How to use: Enter your plate counts for each dilution level in the data table below. The calculator will identify countable plates (within the valid range), average duplicate counts, and compute the final CFU/mL or CFU/g using the standard plate count method. Plates outside the countable range are automatically flagged as TNTC (too numerous to count) or TFTC (too few to count).
