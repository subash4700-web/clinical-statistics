Measurement Uncertainty (MU) BETA

GUM-compliant computation of Expanded Uncertainty (U) from individual uncertainty components, combined in quadrature. Required by ISO 15189 §7.3.4.

1. Uncertainty components

Enter each contribution as a standard uncertainty (uᵢ) in % (relative). Components combine in quadrature: uc = √(u₁² + u₂² + …).

Component u (%)

Bias handling note: ideally, a known bias is corrected before reporting; only the residual uncertainty of the correction enters u_bias. If bias is uncorrected and you want to fold it into U, use u_bias = |Bias| / √3 (rectangular-distribution convention) or simply enter |Bias|.
