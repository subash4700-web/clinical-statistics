EP17-A2 Guidelines:
LoD (Limit of Detection): Lowest concentration where >= 95% of samples test positive.
Minimum replicates: 20 per level recommended. More replicates = narrower confidence intervals.
Confidence interval: Clopper-Pearson (exact binomial) method used for CI calculation.
Negative control: Include a blank/negative level to verify specificity (expect 0% detection).
