Observed vs Expected Counts

Side-by-side comparison of observed counts (blue) and expected counts (orange) for each category.

Accuracy & limitations: The chi-square distribution shown is a mathematical approximation — it becomes unreliable when any expected cell count falls below 5. Expected counts are displayed in the table above; cells below 5 are flagged. With small expected counts, consider combining categories or using a simulation-based p-value rather than the χ² approximation.
